Because cottages like ours are in such demand, especially during high season, we recommend making your reservations as early as possible to ensure that you get the week you want. We're taking reservations now for the 2016 season!


Rental Fees


Peak Season

  • Last week in June through August (one week minimum stay): $2,000.

Pre/Post Peak:

  • June, September: $1,300/week, $200/night.

Shoulder Season:

  • May, October: $1,000/week, $175/night.

Off-Season:

  • November through April: $800/week, $150/night.

**One week minimum in Peak season, two-day minimum all other times.**




One half of the rental fee is due at time of booking. The remainder and damage/cleaning deposit is due 30 days in advance of move-in date.

There is an additional 7% Maine state tax and federal tax.

There is a $250 security deposit, which is completely refundable if the cottage is left in the same condition as when you arrived.

Cancellation Policy: We will refund your money if we can re-rent the cottage for the time you reserved it.

We do allow up to 2 dogs, with permission, at the rate of $125 per dog. No other pets, please.
