Because cottages like ours are in such demand, especially during high season, we recommend making your reservations as early as possible to ensure that you get the week you want!


Rental Fees


Peak Season

  • $2,000 per week. Third week in June until Labor Day Weekend. One week minimum stay.

Pre/Post Peak:

  • June, September: $1,300/week, $200/night.

Shoulder Season:

  • $1,200 per week, $200 per night: For the month of May through the second week in June, and Labor through the end of October. Two day minimum.

Off-Season:

  • November through April: $800 per week, $150 per night. Two day minimum.

**One week minimum in Peak season, two-day minimum all other times.**




One half of the rental fee is due at time of booking. The remainder and damage/cleaning There is an additional 8% Maine state tax and federal tax. There is a $250 security deposit, which is completely refundable if the cottage is left in the same condition as when you arrived.

Cancellation Policy: We will refund your money if we can re-rent the cottage for the time you reserved it.

We do allow up to 2 dogs, with permission, at the rate of $125 per dog. No other pets, please.
