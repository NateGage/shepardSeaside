One half of the rental fee is due at time of booking. The remainder and damage/cleaning deposit is due 30 days in advance of move-in date.

Taxes There is an additional 7% Maine state tax and federal tax.

Damage/Cleaning Deposit There is a $250 deposit, which is completely refundable if the cottage is left in the same condition as when you arrived.

Cancellation Policy If you need to cancel, we will refund your money if we can re-rent the cottage for the time you reserved it.

Pets We do allow up to 2 dogs, with permission, at the rate of $100 per dog. No other pets, please.
