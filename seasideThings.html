There are plenty of activities at the cottage site and in town nearby! Here is a list of some attractions:


  • Enjoy morning coffee with the beautiful sunrise on the water

  • Kayak or canoe on the Castco bay (double Kayak and Canoe available at cottage)

  • Visit the farm animals and activities at Wolfe's Neck Park

  • Watch birds come in and feed at low tide

  • Bike ride up and down the peninsula and to Wolfe's Neck Park (Bikes provided at cottage)

  • Hike and picnic on water at Wolfe's Neck State Park

  • Hike up a small Maine mountain at Bradbury Mountain State Park. Enjoy the view!

  • Swim in the ocean at Winslow Memorial Park

  • Expand your skills through the L.L. Bean Outdoor Discovery School. Several LLBean clinics and paddling school are just up the street!

  • Take a ferry boat from the Dolphin in South Harpswell to Eagle Island State Park

  • Walk down the quiet road and trail to the tip of the peninsula. Pose for photos on the striking rocks.

  • Cook lobsters in the lobster pot and enjoy on the picnic table overlooking the water.

  • Roast marshmallows on the outdoor fire pit overlooking the ocean

  • Walk to back of property and enjoy the colorful sunset reflecting on the Castco bay

  • A rainy day? Appreciate art at the Bowdoin College Museum of Art

  • Enjoy an excellent musical at Maine State Music Theatre (Bowdoin college in Brunswick)

  • In the mood for classical music? - check out the The International Music Festival (Summer - Bowdoin College in Brunswick.)

  • Check out the many museums, galleries and tours in Freeport and Brunswick. (See book in cottage and TripAdvisor.com)

  • Shop the sales in the many outlets and shops in Downtown Freeport
    Freeport USA (http://www.freeportusa.com) provides great list of things to do and see and a schedule of events in town (For example, the wonderful Sparkle Parade in December). The cottage has a book with several brochures with ideas for local trips (Seal and whale watching trips, The Children's Museum in Portland, maps and sites in Camden, Rockland, Bar Harbor, Boothbay etc.)

    • We keep a tidal chart in our welcome book too!
