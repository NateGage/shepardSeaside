Where can I buy lobsters to eat?

Many restaurants serve lobster. If you would like to dine on lobsters outside by the Castco bay, then one option is to eat them by the wharf at the end of Maine Street in South Freeport. This casual eatery is run by the Harraseeket Lunch and Lobster Company and is located three miles from LLBean.
http://www.harraseeketlunchandlobster.com/directions.html
(207) 865-4888

For purchasing lobster by the pound, the following are options:

Day's Crabmeat and Lobster Inc. (Limited hours but often the best prices)
1269 US Route One Yarmouth, Maine | Take Out 207-846-3436 | Pound
207-846-5871
http://www.dayscrabmeatandlobster.com/menu.html

Bowes Street Market (See grocery stores)

Shaws Market (See grocery stores)

Allen's Seafood in Harpswell (at the warf) and Cantrell's Seafood in Topsham are also popular places for buying lobster and other fresh seafood. (But a bit further away.)

Where can I go fishing? How do I get a license?

Maine offers a great many places for fishing depending on just where you want to go. Check out places online or stop into LLBean's Fishing and Hunting store and speak with the helpful staff there.

LLBean Hunting and Fishing
95 Maine Street
1-877-755-2326

Many Maine visitors have enjoyed taking part in a fishing tour. A partial list may be found at http://www.go-maine.com/Brunswick-Freeport/Fishing/

Fishing licenses may be purchased at LLBean Fishing and Hunting store, tackle shops, Town Hall or online through the M.O.S.E.S. (Maine Online Sportsman Electronic System) site.
https://www4.informe.org/cgi-bin/online/moses2/index.pl

May I hold a wedding on the property?

We are often asked this question and are grateful that our property is wedding worthy! Unfortunately due to the septic system and neighborhood restrictions, our cottage cannot accommodate any large groups. (Weddings may be held at nearby Wolfe's Neck Farm.)

Do I need to bring anything for my week?

Food:
Linens, paper products, cleaning products, soaps and laundry detergent are supplied. Just bring food. Feel free to use up any items in cupboard (coffees, tea, canned goods and cooking items left behind by guests we leave for others who may want to use them.)

Jackets:
In summer time the peninsula can be much cooler than town due to the surrounding water temperature, so we recommend you bring jackets warmer than you would typically think needed for that time of year.

Bikes are provided. However, if you chose to bring your own bike, you may store it in the locked shed.
